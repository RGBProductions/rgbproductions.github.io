About Me

I am most proficient in Lua for LÖVE, but I also work with JavaScript and Python, among others. I'm also strongly interested in Game Boy games and development.

Educational Background

I am currently attending Gateway High School, Clovis Community Day School (CCDS), and the Center for Advanced Research and Technology (CART). At CART, I am participating in the User Experience (UX) Design lab, where I have researched, defined, ideated, designed, and tested mobile applications for Jurassic Park and Raising Cane's.

Future Plans

I plan to attend Fresno City College in order to study computer science and fully launch my software and game development career. After college, I may work for smaller game development teams, although I would most likely prefer to work on my own or in my own small team.
